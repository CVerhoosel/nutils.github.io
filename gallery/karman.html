Laminar flow around a cylinder (Reynolds 1000)

Solution of the incompressible Navier-Stokes in the Reynolds=1000 regime, showing Kármán vortices developing in the wake of the cylinder. Solved using the isogeometric finite element method using compatible veloxity/pressure spaces for pointwise divergence free flow fields, and weak imposition of (tangential) no-slip. See the nutils example "cylinderflow" for full implementational details.
