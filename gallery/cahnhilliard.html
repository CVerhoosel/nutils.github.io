Separation of immiscible fluids in a cylinder

Solution of the 3D Cahn-Hilliard equation (without convection) in a stationary cylinder, simulating the process of phase separation of immiscible fluids through diffusion under the influence of surface tension. The blue and yellow manifolds tightly enclose the regions of uniform mixing and can be thought of as the boundaries of the two phases, which are not otherwise visualized.
