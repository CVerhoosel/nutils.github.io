--- layout: post title: Laminar flow around a cylinder thumb: karman ---

Solution of the incompressible Navier-Stokes in different flow regimes, showing Kármán vortices developing in the wake of the cylinder. Solved using the isogeometric finite element method using compatible velocity/pressure spaces for pointwise divergence free flow fields, and weak imposition of (tangential) no-slip. See the nutils example "cylinderflow" for full implementational details.
